Matyas Prokop, Principle Architect @ Natilik

Starting off as a founder of Bubakov.net (a community-led ISP) whilst still in his teens, seeing the potential of a good network, is in Matyas’ DNA. He has since designed data centers at Google and now stands as Principal Architect at Natilik focusing on datacenters, network automation and DevOps. Alongside this he organizes Network Automation Meet-Ups in London, speaking multiple times at Cisco Live and DevNet Create in US and constantly pushing boundaries and supporting this ever-evolving and exciting industry.
